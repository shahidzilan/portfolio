About Me

A bit about myself

I'm Shahid Zilan, a dedicated Computer Science & Engineering student with an unwavering passion for technology and software development. My journey into programming started with curiosity and quickly evolved into a deep love for building things that matter.

Python was my first real programming language, and it opened the door to an incredible world of possibilities — from automating repetitive tasks to exploring the foundations of Artificial Intelligence. Every line of code I write brings me closer to understanding how technology can solve real-world problems.

I believe in continuous learning. Whether it's mastering data structures, exploring machine learning algorithms, or building web applications, I'm always pushing myself to grow. My goal is to become a well-rounded software engineer who can bridge the gap between innovative ideas and practical solutions.

Beyond code, I enjoy problem-solving and creative thinking. I'm fascinated by how AI is reshaping industries and want to be at the forefront of that transformation. As digital threats grow more sophisticated, I've also developed a strong interest in cybersecurity — exploring network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking to understand how to build systems that are not just intelligent but also secure.
